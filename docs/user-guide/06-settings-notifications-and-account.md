# Settings, Notifications, And Account

## Open Settings

1. Open the account menu in the top-right corner.
2. Select `Settings`.
3. Use the tabs to switch between `Account`, `Notifications`, and `Theme`.

## Change Theme

1. Open `Settings`.
2. Select the `Theme` tab.
3. Choose one of these options:

- `System` to follow your device setting.
- `Light` to force the light theme.
- `Dark` to force the dark theme.

### What Happens Next

- The theme changes immediately.
- Your choice is saved to your user profile.
- The same preference is restored after reloads and future sign-ins.

## Change Notification Preferences

1. Open `Settings`.
2. Select the `Notifications` tab.
3. Find the team you want to change.
4. Turn `Mute` on to stop notifications for that team.
5. Turn `Mute` off to restore notifications for that team.

## How In-App Notifications Work

QuorumQ can show toast notifications with deep links for these events.

- A lunch session starts.
- Voting starts.
- A winner is decided.
- The toast action opens the related session or winner screen.

### On The Session Page

- The 5-minute warning appears inline on the session page.
- It does not appear as a separate toast while you are already on that session.

## Account Screen

The `Account` tab shows:

- Your email address
- Your display name
- The `Danger zone` for account deletion

## Delete Your Account

1. Open `Settings`.
2. Select the `Account` tab.
3. In `Danger zone`, select `Delete account`.
4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog.
5. QuorumQ signs you out and returns you to the sign-in page.

### What Deletion Does

- Your account is immediately anonymized in the app as `Former Member`.
- Your old credentials stop working.
- A background cleanup job permanently removes deleted users after 30 days.
