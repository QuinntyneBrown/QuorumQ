# Suggestions, Voting, And Comments

## Suggest A Restaurant

1. Open an active session that is still in `Suggesting`.
2. Expand `Suggest a restaurant`.
3. Enter the restaurant name.
4. Optionally fill in cuisine, address, and website URL.
5. Select `Suggest`.
6. The restaurant appears in the suggestion list for everyone in the session.

## Reuse A Past Restaurant

1. Open the suggestion form during `Suggesting`.
2. Type at least 2 characters into the restaurant name field.
3. Review the autocomplete suggestions from your team's past restaurants.
4. Select a previous restaurant.
5. QuorumQ prefills the saved name, cuisine, address, and website URL.

## If You Try To Suggest A Duplicate

1. Enter a restaurant name that already exists in the current session.
2. QuorumQ shows an `Already suggested by ...` warning.
3. Use the existing suggestion card instead of creating another copy.

## Withdraw Your Own Suggestion

1. Stay in the `Suggesting` phase.
2. Find the suggestion you created.
3. Select the delete icon on that card.
4. Confirm the withdrawal.
5. QuorumQ removes the suggestion for everyone in real time.

### Limits

- You can only withdraw your own suggestion.
- The withdraw action disappears once the session enters `Voting`.

## Vote For A Restaurant

1. Wait until the session enters `Voting`.
2. Find the restaurant you want.
3. Select its vote button.
4. QuorumQ highlights your vote and updates the tally for everyone.

## Change Or Clear Your Vote

### Move Your Vote

1. During `Voting`, select a different restaurant's vote button.
2. QuorumQ moves your vote to the new restaurant and updates both tallies.

### Clear Your Vote

1. During `Voting`, select the same restaurant you already voted for.
2. QuorumQ removes your vote and resets that tally.

### During A Tie-Break

- Only tied restaurants stay votable.
- Non-tied restaurants remain visible but their vote buttons are disabled.

## Comment On A Suggestion

1. Open a suggestion card in an active session.
2. Select `Comments`.
3. Enter a message up to 500 characters.
4. Select `Post`.
5. QuorumQ adds the comment to the thread and shows it to everyone in real time.

## Edit Your Comment

1. Open the comment thread.
2. Select the edit icon on your own comment.
3. Change the text.
4. Select `Save`.
5. QuorumQ updates the comment and adds an `edited` badge.

### Limit

- The backend only allows comment edits for 5 minutes after the original post.

## Delete Your Comment

1. Open the comment thread.
2. Select the delete icon on your own comment.
3. QuorumQ replaces the text with `Comment deleted`.

## Comment Availability

- Comments are available while the session is active.
- Once the session is `Decided` or `Cancelled`, the compose box disappears and the thread becomes read-only.
